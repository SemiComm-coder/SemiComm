Semiconductor Training & Technical Workshops

Practical, industry-led training designed to build technical expertise across semiconductor manufacturing, from foundational concepts to advanced process technologies.

Technical Training

  • Semiconductor lithography, photoresist chemistry (i-line, KrF, ArF & EUV), advanced patterning, photomasks, and advanced packaging.
  • Equipment operation, process integration, Statistical Process Control (SPC), Design of Experiments (DOE), and material qualification.

Professional Development Workshops

  • Customized workshops for engineers, technicians, managers, and non-technical professionals.
  • On-site, virtual, or tailored programmes focused on operational excellence and knowledge transfer.

Semiconductor Process Troubleshooting

Expert support to identify root causes, resolve manufacturing challenges, and improve yield, quality, and process stability.

Process & Yield Improvement

  • Root cause analysis, yield enhancement, process optimization, and manufacturing stability.
  • SPC implementation, process audits, corrective actions (CAPA), and data-driven problem solving.

Lithography & Defect Resolution

  • Troubleshooting resist defects including adhesion, footing, scumming, particles, blobs, and CD variation.
  • Photoresist handling, storage, qualification, and process optimization.
