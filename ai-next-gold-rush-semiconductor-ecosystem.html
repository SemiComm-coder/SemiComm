AI's Next Gold Rush: It's Not Just About GPUs

Samsung and SK Hynix's reported US$1.3 trillion investment signals a new era for the entire semiconductor ecosystem.

Published: July 1, 2026

When most people think about AI, they think about powerful GPUs. But behind every AI breakthrough is an entire semiconductor supply chain working to keep up. Samsung and SK Hynix's reported US$1.3 trillion investment is a clear sign that the race is now about much more than computing power. It is about memory, advanced packaging, manufacturing capacity, and the materials that make it all possible.

For the industry, this is more than another investment announcement. It is a reminder that the future of AI will depend on innovations across the semiconductor value chain. From EUV lithography and photoresists to process engineering and resilient supply chains, the companies that invest in both technology and expertise will be the ones leading the next chapter of semiconductor innovation.
