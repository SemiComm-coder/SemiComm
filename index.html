Why are we starting SEMICOMM

Semiconductor technology is moving fast — but the information around it is often scattered between deep technical papers, market reports, and surface-level commentary. What’s missing is a place where these layers actually come together.

This Substack is where we collect and connect interesting developments in semiconductor technology, materials, process innovation, and the broader market forces shaping the industry.

A bit about who is behind this

This is a collaboration between three perspectives across the semiconductor value chain.

Between us, we bring experience in advanced lithography, semiconductor materials and chemistry, and supply chain strategy for critical raw materials. That mix gives us a view that spans from fundamental process development all the way to the realities of scaling and sourcing materials in production.

We’re not approaching this from a single angle — but from the connection points between process, materials, and industry execution, where the most interesting shifts tend to happen.

What to expect

We’re not trying to cover every headline or product announcement. Instead, we’ll focus on the developments that genuinely catch our attention—and explain why they matter.

Here you’ll find:

  • Technical breakthroughs that are shaping the future of semiconductor technology.
  • Materials and process innovations, from lithography to advanced packaging and beyond.
  • Market and supply chain insights that help explain where the industry is heading.
  • Connections between technology and business, because the most interesting stories rarely exist in isolation.

Whether it’s a new research paper, an emerging manufacturing trend, or a shift in the semiconductor ecosystem, our goal is to provide context—not just news.

In short...

This is a small, early project built from shared experience and curiosity about where the industry is heading.
